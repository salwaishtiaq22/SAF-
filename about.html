About Us

Shahid Afridi Foundation (SAF) was founded by Shahid Afridi, a Pakistani Cricketer popularly known as "BOOM BOOM" worldwide. Our aim is to improve the conditions of underprivileged communities in terms of Education, Access to Water and Food, Healthcare Services, Sports Rehabilitation.

Mission

To enable access to education, healthcare services, and clean water across Pakistan, empowering underprivileged communities for a better future.

Our Vision

We aspire to become the source for transforming lives of the underprivileged communities in Pakistan and across the globe.
